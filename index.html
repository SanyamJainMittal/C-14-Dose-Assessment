§ 4
Pathway III — Animal Products milk & meat via feed equilibrium
▸
Capf = Cair × fc × Sa ⁄ Sair
Animal products inherit the SA ratio of feed, which itself equilibrates with atmospheric CO2. The factor fc allows for animals being fed supplementary concentrates, stored hay/silage, or feed imported from outside the impacted area. For stored feed, the concentration of relevance is that in the stored material at the time of consumption. Set fc from local farming practices; in the absence of site-specific data, use 1.0 (conservative).
Cow Milk
Sa = 65 g C kg-1 FW▸
default: 65.
Indian default (NIN).
Overrides feed-equilibrium Capf. Conversion: Capf = Ap × Sa/1000 (fc bypassed).

default: 170 (goat meat).

Overrides feed-equilibrium Capf. Conversion: Capf = Ap × Sa/1000.
§ 5
Pathway IV — Fish DIC equilibrium
▸
CDIC = Cw ⁄ SDIC  ·  Cf = CDIC × Sf
Fish are assumed to attain full SA equilibrium with the dissolved inorganic carbon (DIC) pool of the water body. Default SDIC = 20 g C L-1 for both; use site-measured values where available. Note: if both freshwater and marine fish are included with the same Hp, the total intake is double-counted — split Hp between the two items according to local consumption pattern, or untick whichever pathway does not apply.
